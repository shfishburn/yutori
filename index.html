Body Composition Calculator

This calculator creates your personalized fitness roadmap by combining proven scientific formulas with your unique data. It starts by calculating your Resting Metabolic Rate using the gold-standard Mifflin-St Jeor equation, then factors in your activity level to determine your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE).

By analyzing your lean mass and target body fat percentage, it optimizes your macronutrient targets and establishes a healthy goal weight range. Interactive forecast charts visualize your projected progress, automatically adjusting to show how changes in your nutrition and activity will impact your journey—giving you a clear, science-backed path to achieving your fitness goals safely and sustainably.
